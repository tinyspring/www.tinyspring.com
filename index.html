Welcome to Tiny Spring project

The minimalistic dependency injection framework based on Spring

Why you should use it?

Maybe you just hate including 1MB+ of Spring jars to get simple DI working with your 10KBs of code?

Or you don't like the way how is your Android project tightly coupled to Roboguice?

Or you hate your Spring MVC app start up time on GAE?

Features

  • small foot print
  • XML configuration (yes it's a feature)
  • modular (Android & Web modules)
  • simple
  • opensource

Download & Participate

The source is available on Github - your help and comments are really welcome!
